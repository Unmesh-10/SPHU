WIDE RANGE OF PRODUCTS

SPHU provides high end manufacturing services are a perfect complement to today’s high tech industries.

PCD Inserts

PCD (POLEX) diamond tool blanks feature a laminated structure with a polycrystalline diamond layer on a tungsten carbide substrate, created through high-pressure, high-temperature processes. This results in excellent durability, making them ideal for cutting and milling. SPHU PCD Tools offer high material removal rates and fine surface finishes for nonferrous and nonmetallic materials, with superior thermal conductivity for efficient heat dissipation.

CBN Tools

SPHU CBN tools, sintered under ultra-high pressure and temperature, feature CBN with a special ceramic bond. This gives them superior hardness and heat resistance, making them ideal for cutting hardened steel and cast iron. They also provide longer tool life in high-speed finishing applications.

SPHU Diamond Dresser

We offer a comprehensive range of premium Diamond Dressers, meticulously crafted from carefully selected high-grade diamonds. These tools are designed to deliver exceptional durability and precision, making them ideal for a wide array of industrial applications.
